About

Hello, we're menta

We've spent the last ten years photographing weddings across England — in barns, gardens, village halls and the occasional clifftop. What keeps us coming back is always the same thing: the small, unrepeatable moments that happen between people who love each other.

Our approach is calm and unobtrusive. We won't line you up for hours of formal portraits or ask you to fake a laugh. We'd rather wait for the real one — it's always better.

When we're not at a wedding you'll find us walking the dog somewhere muddy, or printing photographs in our little studio near Stow-on-the-Wold.
